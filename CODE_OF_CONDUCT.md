# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Respect differing experience levels, identities, and viewpoints.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and correct mistakes.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, personal
attacks, and publishing another person's private information are not allowed.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner using a private
contact method listed on the
[`agporto` GitHub profile](https://github.com/agporto). Reports will be
reviewed promptly and kept confidential to the extent practical.
